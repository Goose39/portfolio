Shaun Gouws

Full Stack Software Engineer

Growing up in South Africa I have always been interested in technology. From the first time I used a computer, at the age of 9 and attempted to connect to the internet (using 56k dial-up modem). I was intrigued by the noise it made but fascinated by all the possibilities that technology provided. Once I was exposed to my first programming language (Delphi 7) in High School I quickly realized the value of being able to write code and create my own applications.

While working in the online gaming and sports betting industry, I was able to get a real understanding of how to integrate a variety of technologies in order to provide customers with online services. I knew that I had to jump over to the other side and get my hands dirty and start coding it myself. The journey of moving from a user of technology, to now implementing the development skills that I had acquired over the years and becoming a creator of that technology, excited me.

I now look forward to working with passionate and like-minded technology experts, motivated to build applications that will challenge traditional thinking and carve their own path.

The backend creates random matches matches for multiple sports and leagues. After a random winner is selected using the given odds, bets are then settled accordingly.
